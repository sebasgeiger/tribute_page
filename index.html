Heads

ECD's heads are top of the line and have different options for different types of players and positions. Heads range in shape and stiffness depending on the type of player the head is designed for. ECD has expanded their head options including faceoff and goalie specific heads as well. You can get heads in multiple colors and prestrung with a customized pocket.

Shafts

ECD's shafts all carry the Carbon name because they are made of carbon fiber which allows them to flex when you shoot. However, even though the shafts flex, they remain durable and do not dent or break. Their flex and flexpoints range based on player's preference. They also have long poles for defenseman and goalie specific shafts too.

Mesh + Strings

ECD's mesh is made Repel + Coating technology which prevents the mesh's performance to be affected in wet conditions. It also prevents the mesh from bagging out and making a players stick illegal. The strings are woven tightly to prevent them from ripping or loosening over time. They also come pre-cut and with solid tips making it easy to string.
